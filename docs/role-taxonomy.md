# Role Taxonomy

Growth Marketing Jobs focuses on growth, go-to-market, and startup marketing roles.

The site is not limited to only narrow “growth marketing” titles. It also includes adjacent marketing roles that commonly sit close to acquisition, positioning, launches, demand generation, lifecycle, customer expansion, content, community, and revenue growth.

The goal is to make marketing roles easier to browse when someone is looking for work connected to startup growth, measurable marketing, or go-to-market execution.

## Core role families

### Growth Marketing

Roles focused on finding, testing, and scaling growth channels.

Example titles:

- Growth Marketing Manager
- Senior Growth Marketing Manager
- Growth Lead
- Growth Manager
- Growth Marketing Specialist
- Head of Growth

### Product Marketing / PMM

Roles focused on positioning, messaging, product launches, competitive intelligence, sales enablement, and go-to-market strategy.

Example titles:

- Product Marketing Manager
- Senior Product Marketing Manager
- PMM
- Growth Product Marketing Manager
- Go-to-Market Manager
- Product Marketing Lead
- Director of Product Marketing

### Demand Generation

Roles focused on pipeline, campaigns, lead generation, account-based marketing, and revenue-focused marketing programs.

Example titles:

- Demand Generation Manager
- Demand Gen Manager
- Lead Generation Manager
- ABM Manager
- Campaign Marketing Manager
- Revenue Marketing Manager
- Integrated Campaigns Manager

### Performance Marketing / Paid Acquisition

Roles focused on paid acquisition, paid media, campaign performance, CAC, ROAS, trials, demos, pipeline, or revenue.

Example titles:

- Performance Marketing Manager
- Paid Acquisition Manager
- Paid Media Manager
- Digital Marketing Manager
- User Acquisition Manager
- Acquisition Marketing Manager
- Paid Social Manager
- Paid Search Manager
- SEM Manager

### SEO / Content / Organic Growth

Roles focused on organic search, technical SEO, programmatic SEO, content growth, editorial strategy, or organic acquisition.

Example titles:

Example titles:

Example titles:

- Web Experimentation Manager

### Brand / Content / Communications-Adjacent Marketing

Some brand, content, and communications-adjacent roles may fit when they are clearly connected to marketing execution, audience growth, launches, campaigns, or go-to-market work.

Example titles:

- Integrated Marketing Manager

- Head of Marketing

- Chief Marketing Officer
- Director of Growth Marketing
- Director of Demand Generation

- VP Growth

## Lower-priority or excluded roles

Some roles may be excluded unless the title or description clearly connects them to marketing, growth, or go-to-market work.

Lower-priority examples:

- Sales roles
- Account executive roles
- Customer success roles without customer marketing ownership
- Product manager roles
- Product designer roles
- Engineering roles
- Recruiting or talent roles
- Finance roles
- Legal roles
- Support roles
- Pure communications or PR roles with no marketing ownership
- General operations roles with no marketing ownership

## Classification goal

The goal is a useful marketing-job index, not a perfect ontology.

Growth Marketing Jobs should include roles that are meaningfully relevant to startup marketing, growth, go-to-market, acquisition, positioning, lifecycle, customer expansion, content, community, analytics, and marketing operations.

That means the taxonomy should stay broad enough to reflect real startup marketing teams, but focused enough to avoid becoming a generic job board for every business role.
